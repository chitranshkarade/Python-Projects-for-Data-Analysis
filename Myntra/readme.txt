## Myntra Sale Dataset
link - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/manishmathias/myntra-fashion-dataset

This repository contains the Myntra Sale dataset, which provides information about products available for sale on the Myntra e-commerce platform. The dataset offers valuable insights into various aspects of the products, such as brands, categories, prices, discounts, ratings, and customer reviews.

### Dataset Details

The dataset consists of the following columns:

- `Product_id`: A unique identifier for each product.
- `Brandsnew`: The brand name associated with the product.
- `Category`: The general category or type of the product.
- `INdi_category_New`: A more specific category or sub-category of the product.
- `category_by_Gender`: The category of the product based on the target gender (e.g., men, women, unisex).
- `SizeOption`: Information about the available size options for the product.
- `OriginalPrice (in Rs)`: The original price of the product before any discount is applied, in Indian Rupees.
- `Discounted_price`: The sale price of the product after the discount has been applied.
- `Discountpercent`: The percentage of discount applied to the original price.
- `Total_discount`: The total amount saved on the product, calculated as the difference between the original price and the discounted price.
- `Ratings`: The rating assigned to the product by customers, indicating overall satisfaction or quality.
- `Reviews`: Textual reviews or feedback given by customers for the product.

### Potential Applications

This dataset can be useful for various data analysis and machine learning tasks, including but not limited to:

- Exploratory data analysis to understand customer preferences, popular brands, and categories.
- Analyzing pricing strategies, discounts, and their impact on sales.
- Studying customer ratings and reviews to identify trends and sentiments.
- Building predictive models to estimate product prices or customer ratings based on available features.
